Cookies Recipe

Description

These cookies are soft, chewy, and packed with chocolate chips. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, add the melted butter, the brown sugar and the granulated sugar. Mix then
  2. Add the egg and the egg yolk, then mix
  3. Add the vanilla extract and mix
  4. In another bowl, mix the flour, baking soda and salt
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips
  7. Make cookies and let them rest in the refrigerator for 30 minutes
  8. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C)
  9. Place the cookies on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  10. Bake for 15-17 minutes or until the edges are golden brown
  11. Let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack
